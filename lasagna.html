Lasagna

Lasagna is delicious. Today we will learn how to make it. It's not easy, but it is scrumtrulescent.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Noods
  • oregano
  • meat (like lamb or beef or something>

Steps

  1. First you go to the store and buy all the stuff
  2. Next put it all together in a pan
  3. You should have already made the oven hot.
  4. Put it all in the oven for a while.
